There are a number of symbol replacements aimed at simple typesetting as well. ‘Speech marks’ automatically become left and right versions (and single, due to the author’s British English upbringing — use “double” for double quotes, although this can be changed). Three hyphens — em dash, two – en. Ellipsis… Crossed out bold text.
